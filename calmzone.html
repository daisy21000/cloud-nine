Find Serenity and Calm

If you are stressed, here are some ways to help you relax

Head outside and enjoy the serenity of nature, away from the noise and stress. Calming environments can help you relax and reduce stress.

When stressed, it's beneficial to get yourself busy and have some fun doing things such as playing a video game or reading a book - whatever you enjoy! Distracting yourself from your worries can reduce stress.

Mindfulness is slowing down and paying attention to the present moment - your thoughts and feeling and the world around you. This can reduce stress and improve your mental wellbeing.

It doesn't have to be a full workout - even a short walk can help reduce stress, and exercise helps self-esteem too.

It can be hard when you're overwhelmed. Take things one at a time and don't rush. Trying to do everything at once will induce further stress.

Don't keep everything to yourself. Talking about your problems to someone you trust, like a friend or family member, can help with your stress and help you get the support you need.
