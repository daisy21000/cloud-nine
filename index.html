About Us

At Cloud Nine, we believe that mental health support should be gentle, accessible, and stigma-free. Our goal is to offer calm, trustworthy advice that helps you feel seen, supported, and empowered — whether you're navigating stress, anxiety, or simply seeking a moment of peace.

Our Mission

We're here to make mental health guidance feel approachable and human. By sharing simple, evidence-informed advice in a serene space, we hope to help you reconnect with your inner calm and take small steps toward emotional well-being.

Why It Matters

Mental health affects every part of life, yet many people feel unsure where to begin. We created Cloud Nine to be a quiet starting point — a place where you can pause, breathe, and explore advice that meets you where you are.

Get The Support You Need

Contact these organizations for more help and support

Mind

Mind is a UK-based mental health charity offering information, support, and advocacy for people experiencing mental health challenges. They provide resources on a wide range of conditions and campaign to improve mental health services and awareness.

info@mind.org.uk

0300 123 3393

https://www.mind.org.uk/

Samaritans

Samaritans is a confidential support service available 24/7 for anyone in emotional distress or crisis. You can talk to a trained volunteer at any time — no judgment, no pressure, just someone who listens.

jo@samaritans.org

116 123

https://www.samaritans.org/

Young Minds (For Young People)

Young Minds is a leading UK charity focused on children and young people’s mental health. They offer advice for young people, parents, and professionals, and work to ensure that mental health support is accessible and youth-centred.

85258

https://www.youngminds.org.uk/

NHS Mental Health Support

NHS Mental Health services provide free, professional support across the UK, including therapy, crisis care, and referrals. Their website offers trusted information on mental health conditions and how to access help through the NHS.

111

https://www.nhs.uk/mental-health/
